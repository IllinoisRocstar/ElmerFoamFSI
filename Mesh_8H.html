Square Edges:   12 23 34 41 (ccw)
Tet Faces:      132 241 342 143
Pyr Faces:      1432 251 352 453 154
Prism Faces:    2541 3652 1463 132 456
Hex Faces:      1432 2651 3762 4873 1584 5678
